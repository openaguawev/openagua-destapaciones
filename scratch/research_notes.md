# Research Notes: Openagua SEO Growth Audit

## Current State Analysis
- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router)
- **Content Strategy**: Reactive blog (10+ articles), PSEO (100+ barrios), Core Services (9).
- **Interlinking**: Found links from blog to services, and barrios to services.
- **On-Page SEO**: Good technical baseline, linked JSON-LD Schema (Organization/LocalBusiness/Service).

## Competitor Landscape (Buenos Aires / GBA)
1. **Aggregators**: Mercado Libre (ranking for "destapaciones [barrio]"), Houzz, Home Solution.
2. **Niche Specialized**: 
   - `destapacionesaires24hs.com.ar`
   - `destapacioneszonanorte.com.ar`
   - `destapacionesenelactobsas.com.ar`
3. **Common Tactics**: Heavy focus on "24 hs", "Urgencias", "Atención en el día", "Precios bajos".

## High-Volume Keyword Gaps (Transactional)
- **Price Queries**: "precio destapaciones de cloacas", "cuanto sale destapar un caño", "precio por metro destapaciones".
- **Urgency Queries**: "destapaciones urgencias capital federal", "destapaciones 24 hs zona oeste".
- **B2B Queries**: "destapaciones para edificios", "abonos para consorcios", "limpieza de trampas de grasa para restaurantes".
- **Specific Problem Queries**: "inodoro tapado desborda", "cañeria pluvial llena de tierra".

## Search Intent Gaps
- **Commercial Intent**: Many blog posts are "How-to" (DIY). While good for top-funnel, they need clearer conversion paths for "I've tried everything, I need a pro NOW".
- **Local Intent**: Barrio pages are good but could benefit from "Service + Barrio" landing combinations (e.g., "Destapacion de Cloacas en Palermo").

## Interlinking Weaknesses
- **Blog to Barrio**: Very few links from informational posts to specific high-traffic neighborhood pages.
- **Service to Case Study**: No links to "Real Works" (NuestrosTrabajos) from within service details to build trust.
- **Internal Loop**: Lack of "Related Articles" section in blog detail to keep users on-site.
